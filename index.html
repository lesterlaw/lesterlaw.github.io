What we do

We are a platform to help people increase sales productivity by providing them with a range of products to choose from.

We aim to build a team of salespeople who are constantly on the ground selling, who constantly help businesses push their sales sky-high. This will be a win-win for business and everyday people like you and me.

How it works

View products

View and choose the products from our list of products from different clients. Products have different categories and commisison rates.

Be a seller

Indicate that you are interested in being a seller of the product. You can choose to be a seller to multiple products.

Sell the product

Go out and sell the products that you are interested in. Whether it is door-to-door or telemarketing, you make the decision!

Get your commission

After closing the sale, tell us the customers' information and we will get you the commission shortly!
